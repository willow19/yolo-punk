Our Initiatives

Helping society in big small ways

For a tree to exist, someone should have sown a seed, put manure, tended the soil and watered the sapling, and on the same lines we have sown the seeds of social change with our initiatives and are tending to them so that our initiatives just like the branches and roots of a tree can spread far and wide, reaching out to a large segment of rural India along with reaching out to and involving a large segment of the urban youth to participate in our initiatives.

indiONE

This path breaking initiative strives to bring technology to the bottom of the social pyramid. Root cause analysis has helped us understand that the profit model has limitations of making profits as the bottom of pyramid do not make an attractive target customer for them to pursue. We use our non-profit model to overcome this limitation by using Corporate Social Responsibility, and marketing funds in exchange for tax rebates and cause marketing, to do what we call Technology actualization, which is bringing innovation and frugal technologies to the masses. Our collaborative model of indiOne program enables us to do this. In this model we have a pool of different technologies from our top engineering institute partners and we create a network of other partners to help with the implementation. We then identify the need in the settlement that we can help suffice and involve the technology partners to assess and obtain the techno-commercial proposal. In the next step, we plan a cause marketing campaign with the help of Visibility partners and submit the proposal to our corporate partners, and on receiving their approval for the same, we execute the plan. We also plan how the investment would add to the brand equity of the Corporate funding a project. This is actually a win-win model for everyone involved. In short, we have a Technology Partner, an Activation Partner, a Cause Marketing team, an Implementation team which is ably assisted by enthusiastic Volunteers.

SRP Clinic

SRP Clinic is an online directory of professionals, who have volunteered to provide pro bono service. The SRP Clinic is also a part of SRP Connect, as a person can redeem the social equity points earned by doing any social work, by availing the services of a professional listed in the directory, and the professional too will receive social equity points on providing his service, which he can redeem. This actually creates a symbiotic and mutually beneficial network. In order to register with the SRP Clinic, please click on the link below and fill up the application form.

SRP Connect

In our endeavour to promote the concept of PSR (Personal Social Responsibility) i.e. to involve youth in social service, we are developing a mobile app called SRP Connect. With this app we aim to revolutionize the concept and working model of social work, enabling you to do social work with a simple touch on your smart phone. The urban youth is an ocean of skills and currently an untapped resource for social growth. This unique app aims at using this strong pool of resource to do social tasks from the PSR Action board, which lists the active tasks that various NGOs need to execute or complete a certain project. The volunteers can self assign the tasks as per their convenience, skills and competency. On completion of every task, a volunteer earns social equity which can be redeemed by him in either getting a personal task done from the SRP Connect network or by purchasing merchandise from the E-store. This app also features the SRP Clinic, which is an online directory of professionals, who have volunteered to provide Pro bono service.
