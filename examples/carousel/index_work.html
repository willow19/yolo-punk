About Us

SRP is a group of young professionals from different walks of life who firmly believe that constructive efforts, irrespective of their scale, invariably result in value. It is our effort to use our professional skills and education to contribute to bridging the gap between rural and urban India along with using and promoting education as a tool of social change. This passion was channeled into action on 11-11-11 by applying for a legal recognition to the Registrar of Societies, Thane. That was the first step taken, and over the years we have made considerable progress towards realizing this dream, and we strive to make our solutions accessible to the youth, who are the key change makers.

History

We started with a dream, a goal and passion to involve the Indian youth in technology actualization. Since our advent in this arena in 2011, we have made tremendous and relentless efforts in realizing our goal of making education and energy accessible to rural populations along with promoting environmental awareness among the masses. Rome was not built in a day, and our organization is in the process of achieving social change on a large scale magnitude. We strive to make our place in history by using technology to reach out to people in need, thereby making technology both a tool and the solution to resolving social problems.
